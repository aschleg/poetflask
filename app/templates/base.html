Poetflask utilizes the data provided by the PoetryDB API. This is simply a minimal app to allow users to explore the poetry available in the PoetryDB Database without needing to work with any APIs or databases. All data and credit for the creation and ongoing maintenance of PoetryDB goes to @thundercomb

The poetry icon images used are the work of Alex Manthei.
