About Poetflask

Poetflask is built with Flask with a small Postgresql database that stores the poetry and author data. More information and the source code can be found at the Github repo.

About PoetryDB

PoetryDB was created and is currently maintained by @thundercomb. They blog about poetry and related technology and other topics at thecombedthunderclap.blogspot.com.

Please note, I am in no way affiliated with the creator of PoetryDB. My intention with Poetflask is to help spread the word and hopefully increase interest in poetry and related projects that attempt to create comprehensive databases of publicly available knowledge.
