Poetflask is a simple Flask app that displays information and poetry extracted from various online sources and APIs. The poetry content was extracted from PoetryDB, and the poet descriptions and other information is taken from Wikipedia through the MediaWiki API. The extracted data is stored in a Postgresql database.
